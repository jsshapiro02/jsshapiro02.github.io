Heartswings is a Hispanic DMV post-punk trio formed in early 2025 by three lifelong friends: Jake Blake, Calixto IV, and Neil Cruz. After initially jamming out in local talent shows during their middle and high school days, they have since reunited after college with a dark tone that has been described as both timeless and urgently new. Jake, co-frontman of the darkwave band Social Station (Young & Cold Records), handles vocals and bass, bringing seasoned talent off of U.S. and European tours. Beside him, Calixto leads on guitar; as an independent producer, his remixes have reached more than 50 million viewers across all platforms.
